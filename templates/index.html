About Optocare

Optocare is an online optical store offering you the best optical care from wherever you are. Optocare partners with the best eye care professionals in your area for professional eye care. We provide not only what your eye needs but also what fits you because we believe in you being presentable all the time.

Our clients are:-

  • 1. Individuals who need visual aid,
  • 2. Individuals who need glasses and sunglasses for fashion,
  • 3. Individuals interested in contact lenses for fashion or corrective vision,
  • 4. Eyecare practitioners in need of optical machines and tools,
  • 5. Eyecare practitioners seeking to train their staff with latest optical knowledge,
  • 6. Optical stores seeking to connect with world leading brands,
  • 7. Optical stores seeking to upgrade their machines and equipment.

Operating from UAE gives us an upper hand and deeper international reach, providing consultation services regarding global brands.
